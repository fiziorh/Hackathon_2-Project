Mathematics and Data Science

A course roadmap helps students understand the overall structure of
the course and how each module or topic relates to one another.

It provides a visual representation of the learning journey, allowing
students to see the logical flow of content and how concepts build
upon each other.

The Mathematics and Data Science module emphasizes the integration
of mathematical techniques with data-driven methodologies. Students
will learn how to leverage mathematical models and algorithms to
analyze and extract valuable insights from complex datasets. They will
also acquire proficiency in using programming languages, statistical
software, and data visualization tools commonly employed in data science.

Moreover, the module incorporates hands-on projects and case
studies, enabling students to apply their mathematical and data
science knowledge to practical scenarios. By working on real-world
datasets and tackling data challenges, students will develop critical
thinking skills and problem-solving abilities required in data-driven
decision-making.
