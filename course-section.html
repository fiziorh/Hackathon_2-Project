About course module

The Mathematics and Data Science module is a comprehensive course designed to
provide students with a solid foundation in mathematical principles and their applications
in the field of data science. This module combines the theoretical underpinnings of
mathematics with practical skills in data analysis and interpretation.

Throughout the course, students will explore key topics in mathematics, including
calculus, linear algebra, probability theory, and statistics. They will gain a deep
understanding of mathematical concepts and learn how to apply them to real-world data
problems. The module also covers essential topics in data science, such as data
preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, and predictive modeling.
